The routine

Ninety seconds, morning and night.

Step 01 — Prep

Cleanse & dry

Actives absorb best on clean, fully dry skin. Wait two minutes after cleansing — damp skin amplifies retinal irritation.

Step 02 — Treat

Serum, then eyes

Three drops of Retinal Serum pressed — not rubbed — across face and neck at night. Eye cream morning and night, ring finger only.

Step 03 — Seal

Barrier cream

Finish with Ceramide Recovery Cream to lock actives in and irritation out. In the morning, always follow with SPF.
